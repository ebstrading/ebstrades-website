About Us

EBS Trading LLC is a UAE-based trading company operating primarily in the United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, and other GCC markets. The company focuses on building strong, reputable brands and long-term business relationships across the region.

Our Brand

Delta Wraps is the flagship brand under EBS Trading LLC, specializing in automotive solutions including Paint Protection Films (PPF), Color Vinyl Wraps, and Nano Ceramic Window Films.

Business Philosophy

We believe in transparency, quality-driven products, and sustainable growth. Our objective is to create trust, value, and a strong market presence across the GCC.
